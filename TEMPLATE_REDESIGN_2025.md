## üé® Visual Hierarchy Best Practices

### Name & Title
- **Name:** Largest element (2.25-2.5rem), bold
- **Title:** Below name, slightly smaller, semibold
- **Contact:** Smallest in header, normal weight

### Sections
- **Header:** Bold, all caps or title case, with visual treatment
- **Content:** Normal weight, good line height
- **Sub-items:** Indented or styled distinctly

### Experience Entries
- **Job Title:** Bold, slightly larger
- **Company:** Normal or semibold
- **Dates:** Right-aligned or inline, lighter color
- **Bullets:** Strong action verbs, metrics prominent
